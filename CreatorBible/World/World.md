# World

## Setting

The Leo & Pip stories take place in a warm, safe family home and its immediate surroundings. The world is contemporary, recognisable, and calm.

## Locations

| Registry ID | Name | Background asset | Use |
|-------------|------|------------------|-----|
| `bedroom` | Bedroom | `backgrounds/bedroom.svg` | Leo's room — bed, toys, building |
| `hallway` | Hallway | `backgrounds/hallway.svg` | Cream walls, wooden floor, front door |
| `kitchen` | Kitchen | `backgrounds/kitchen.svg` | Dad calling from kitchen |
| `garden` | Garden | `backgrounds/garden.svg` | Outdoor scenes (future) |

Layer props (spaceship, bricks, cardboard box) on top of `bedroom` — do not create duplicate background variants.

## Visual Environment Rules

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| Walls | Cream `#E8DFD0` or `#FAF3E8` |
| Floor | Warm wood `#D4C4A8` with outline |
| Lighting | Soft, even — no harsh shadows or dark corners |
| Clutter | Minimal — one clear focal point per scene |

## Time of Day

Most stories happen in the morning. Warm morning light is implied through palette, not dramatic sunbeams.

## The Imaginary Layer

Pip exists in the imaginary layer. Adults cannot see Pip. Objects Pip notices are real. Pip's wonderings are real conversations for Leo.

## Scale

- Leo is child-scale relative to doorways and furniture
- Pip is roughly knee-height to Leo
- Whiskers is cat-scale (small but prominent)

## Adding Locations

New locations must be registered in the Asset Registry and documented here before use in any story.
