# Leo

## Identity

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Name | Leo |
| Age | 7 |
| Role | Protagonist — the child the reader recognises themselves in |

## Personality

Leo is curious, warm, and trying his best. He gets absorbed in ideas and projects. He sometimes loses track of time — not because he is naughty, but because his brain follows interesting threads.

**Never draw Leo as naughty, defiant, or being told off.** Adults in stories are gentle and understanding.

## Voice & Dialogue Rules

- Leo speaks in short, honest sentences
- He says what he means ("Okay!" — and he really means it)
- He can be surprised without being ashamed
- Never sarcastic or cynical
