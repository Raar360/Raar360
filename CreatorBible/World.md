# World

## Setting

The Leo & Pip stories take place in a warm, safe family home and its immediate surroundings. The world is contemporary, recognisable, and calm.

## Visual Environment Rules

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| Walls | Cream `#E8DFD0` or `#FAF3E8` |
| Floor | Warm wood `#D4C4A8` with outline |
| Lighting | Soft, even — no harsh shadows or dark corners |
| Clutter | Minimal — one clear focal point per scene |
| Outdoors | Not used in early stories; future expansion |

## Time of Day

- Most stories happen in the morning ("Every morning, before breakfast…")
- Warm morning light implied through palette, not dramatic sunbeams

## The Imaginary Layer

Pip exists in the imaginary layer. Adults cannot see Pip. Objects Pip notices are real (the green brick is really on the floor). Pip's wonderings are real conversations for Leo.

## Story Objects

Physical story objects (green brick, radio, etc.) exist in the real world and may appear in Pip's backpack at story endings. See `Objects.md`.

## Scale

- Leo is child-scale relative to doorways and furniture
- Pip is roughly knee-height to Leo
- Whiskers is cat-scale (small but prominent)

## Future Locations

As the series grows, new locations must be added here before new background assets are authored.
