# Whiskers

## Identity

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Name | Whiskers |
| Role | The family cat — comic relief and gentle warmth |

## Personality

Whiskers is proud, fluffy, and unbothered. He squeezes into boxes, purrs loudly, and accepts salutes from Pip. Whiskers never scratches, hisses, or causes trouble.

| Feature | Specification |

| Eyes | Green `#6BAA6B`, half-closed when purring |
| Outline | Comic outline `#2C1810` |

## Dialogue Rules

- Whiskers speaks only in cat sounds ("Meeooow")
- Other characters interpret Whiskers' role (Captain, etc.)

## Scene Rules

- Whiskers appears in Leo's room scenes
- Pip may salute Whiskers as "Captain Whiskers"
- Whiskers is never in danger or distress
