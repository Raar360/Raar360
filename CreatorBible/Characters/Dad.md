## Personality

Dad is patient, kind, and never cross. He gives reminders ("Shoes on please") as invitations, not commands. When Leo gets absorbed in a project, Dad smiles — he understands.

Dad never lectures. He asks gentle questions: "Weren't you heading somewhere first?"

## Dialogue Rules

- Short, warm sentences
- Never uses "naughty", "bad", or diagnostic language
- Acknowledges Leo's work before redirecting ("It looks amazing.")
- Uses questions rather than corrections
